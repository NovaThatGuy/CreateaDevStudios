Classical VRC

Classical VRC World

This is a work in progress 3D world built for the game VR Chat. It will consist of 3 major rooms, including a spawn area, main room, and balcony area. Rough modeling and design sketches have been made for it already and development is steady.

Devlog Report: 2

Date: 10/28/23

Over the last few days effort towards learning how lighting works in Unity has resulted in significant improvements in the visual quality of the world. Next we intend to work on improving general texture mapping, and fix any noteable lighting based graphical errors that remain in the main room.

Devlog Report: 1

Date: 10/24/23

A week or so has passed since development on this world has begun, and things are moving along slowly but steadily. As of now we have the genral layout and important decoration points done. We still need to texture and fully decorate the building itself and finish working on lighting among other things.
